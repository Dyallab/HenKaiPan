Commercial rollout focus

The next stage is aimed at product, platform and security teams that want faster visibility, smoother remediation workflows and executive-grade reporting without turning rollout into a heavyweight transformation project.

Product direction

Primary offer: managed cloud SaaS. Secondary offer: a first self-hosted edition for organizations that need stronger deployment control, procurement flexibility or internal boundary requirements. Feature parity stays as high as possible across both.
